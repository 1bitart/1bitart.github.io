What are 1-Bit Punks?

1-Bit Punks are Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs), unique digital assets that can take the form of artwork and provide utility to holders.

Which blockchain will this be on?

Solana.

Which Solana wallet can I use?

You can use Phantom, Sollet, Slope and Solflare wallets.

You are free to do anything you want with them under a non-exclusive license.
